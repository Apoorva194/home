Forms of social media and effects

Forms of media Effects Logo
Instagram Instagram is a form of social media that covers the target audience of teens to early adults. Information and stroies rapidly spread on this platform with the help of posts, stories, and hastags.
Snapchat Snapchat targets mostly teenagers, and is much more private and limited.
But the biggest concept with snap chat is filters that can provide information resources and other necessary ideas when dealing with an issue.
Facebook Facebook targets more of the older audience .
Facebook plays a large role with the ability to post pictures, undate your profile picture, shares and tags, as ideas spread very quickly.
Twitter Twitter as well targets an older crowd but is used in a formal manor.
On twitter things spread really quickly as people can share their ideas using tweets which can then be liked commented on and shared.
Electronic news Electronic news is news that is published online for the purpose of sharing the news to the public. On electronic news larger articles and news are shown as articles.
Physical news Paper news is the old fashion way of sharing news Usually only the most important things are shown. Paper news also containes interviews and opinions on certain topics.

How social media plays a role?

Social Media plays a big role on how quickly news and ideas spread. This can be positive and negative.

A quick list showing the pros and cons is below
Pros Cons
