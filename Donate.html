How to Help



Why should I help?


Helping such causes is very important because looking how far humans have come it is not right that certain humans are treated differently due to the way they look. Helping donate to organizations that protect and support those who are beeing racially attacked as it shows public support. Supporting the cause using social media and signing petitions is very important as well becuase it shows public support in numbers and change can be made, and with social media your educating other about this topic.

Where and how can I help


To help you can visit many different web pages to donate. Many people may not be in an optimal financial situation, so that doesnt mean they cannot help. Besides donation petitions can be signed and awareness can be raised using social media.

What is the impact of my help


The impact of your help of any size will a direct impact on the lives of those be racially discriminted. Justice will be brought to those like George Floyd who lost their live due to the shameless act of someone with authority. Further more any help is slowly filling the gap which has been created and will ensure in the future that racial discrimination decreases.


Many creators have taken the intiative to raise awarness, some have decided to help using their platform. An example of this is youtuber Greta Onieogou decided to donate all the add revenue to BLM Toronto so basically a user watching the video is supporting the cause.
